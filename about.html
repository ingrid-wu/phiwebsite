We are the Phi Pledge Class of the Epsilon Chapter of Theta Tau! Comprised of 5 sophomores and 3 juniors at UC Berkeley from a diverse range of engineering backgrounds, we are tackling the task of creating a mailbox for the brothers of Theta Tau.

The Phiiine Box

The Theta Tau Phi Pledge Class Engineering Project is a smart, secure, and weatherproof mailbox. The mailbox will be mounted outside of Tause and will be larger than a traditional home mailbox. This custom mailbox will be synced with Slack and feature motion sensors that will notify Tause residents through the messaging board when a new package or mail is delivered. It will also feature a one-way door for mail drop-off and a lock box on the bottom for mail pick-up. In addition, the mailbox will be coated with protective outdoor paint in order to combat weather damage.

Objective

We want to build a mailbox that provides a safe place for packages and mail to be delivered. The durability of this mailbox will allow it to serve Tause residents for many years to come. Given these goals, our primary focus for this mailbox will be security, followed by the functionality of the Slack notification features.
